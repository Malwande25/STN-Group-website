01 — FORESTRY & CHARCOAL
Black Wattle Charcoal Manufacturing

Manufacturing and supplying high-quality Black Wattle Charcoal sourced and produced in the Eastern Cape. Our product is 100% natural, sustainable, and carries a hotter, cleaner, longer burn — low moisture and low ash — suitable for retail, hospitality, and industrial energy use.

02 — AGRICULTURE & MECHANIZATION
Sustainable Farming & Crop Supply

We supply Maize to local and commercial distributors including Mthiza, and Cabbage Crops to SPAR in Mthatha and East London. Our mechanization services include ploughing, planting, ripping, and spraying — using modern machinery to improve farm productivity and reduce costs.

Bulk Transport & Material Supply

STN Cartage provides reliable, cost-effective transportation of bulk construction and agricultural materials across the Eastern Cape. We deliver building sand, river sand, plaster sand, crushed stone, concrete stone, gravel, gum tree poles, and Sabhunga (red soil) for a wide range of projects.
