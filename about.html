Our Story

About STN Group

A multidisciplinary South African company established in 2005, rooted in excellence, innovation, and community development across the Eastern Cape.

Established 2005

Two Decades of Trusted Service

STN Group (STN Communications CC) was founded in 2005 with a clear purpose: to deliver innovative, reliable, and quality-driven solutions across multiple sectors in South Africa's Eastern Cape — and to do so in a way that uplifts and empowers the communities in which we operate.

Over the past two decades we have diversified from our communications roots into a truly multidisciplinary organisation spanning Agriculture, Forestry & Charcoal Manufacturing, Mechanization, Water Solutions, Construction, Cartage, Technology, and Security.

Our integrated approach, backed by experienced management and community-focused operations, positions us as a trusted partner for public and private sector clients alike.
