About the Division

Local Farming, Commercial Impact

STN Group participates actively in local to commercial farming, supplying agricultural solutions that empower local farmers and distributors across the Eastern Cape.

We grow and supply produce to commercial retailers, provide mechanization services to smallholder and commercial farmers, and actively contribute to strengthening the region's agricultural value chain.

Through mechanization, STN enables farmers to work faster, smarter, and more sustainably — reducing operational costs while achieving consistent, high-quality results.

Mechanization Services

Modern Machinery for Better Farming

STN's agricultural mechanization focuses on improving farm productivity through modern machinery, skilled operators, and efficient land-preparation systems. We help farmers increase output, reduce operational costs, and achieve consistent, high-quality results.

🚜
Ploughing

Deep and shallow ploughing for land preparation and soil aeration.

🌱
Planting

Mechanized planting for accurate seed placement and optimal spacing.

⚙️
Ripping

Subsoil ripping to break compacted layers and improve root penetration.

💦
Spraying

Precision chemical and fertilizer spraying for crop protection and yield.

🔩
Discing

Disc harrow operations for seedbed preparation and residue incorporation.

🛠️
Maintenance

Reliable machinery maintenance and technical supervision on-site.
