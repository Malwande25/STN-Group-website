About the Division

Eastern Cape's Trusted Charcoal Supplier

STN Black Wattle Charcoal is a trusted supplier of premium-quality charcoal produced in the Eastern Cape. Our product is natural, sustainable, and suitable for retail & wholesale markets, restaurants & hospitality, and industrial energy applications.

As a proudly local Eastern Cape brand, we combine responsible harvesting practices with quality-tested manufacturing to deliver a product that performs and a business model that benefits our communities.

Markets & Applications
