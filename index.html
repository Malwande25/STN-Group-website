STN Charcoal Division

Black Wattle Charcoal Manufacturing

STN Black Wattle Charcoal is a trusted supplier of premium-quality charcoal produced in the Eastern Cape. Our product is natural, sustainable, and suitable for retail & wholesale markets, restaurants & hospitality, and industrial energy applications.

We handle charcoal production & packaging, bulk supply & distribution, quality testing (Moisture, Ash, Fixed Carbon, Calorific Value), sustainable wood sourcing, and are undergoing SABS certification process support.

STN Agricultural Produce

From the Field
to Your Table

We participate actively in local to commercial farming and supply agricultural solutions that empower local farmers & distributors. We supply Maize to local and commercial distributors including Mthiza, Eastern Cape.

We supply Cabbage Crops to commercial retailers including SPAR in Mthatha and East London, Eastern Cape.

Our agricultural mechanization covers ploughing, planting, ripping, and spraying — using modern machinery and skilled operators to improve farm productivity and reduce costs.
